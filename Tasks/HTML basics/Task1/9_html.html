LINEAR EQUATIONS

An equation that has maximum degree one is known as Linear Equations. The graph of it is a straight line.
For example,

  • y = 8x - 2
  • ax1 + bx2 - cx3 = 9
are some examples of a linear equation in two and three variables.

Slope of intersection of two Linear Equations:
y1 = mx + a and
y2 = mx2 + b
for the most points P(x1,y1) and Q(x2,y2) is:
m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1)
